Hi! I am postdoctoral scholar in the WiSeCom group at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Spain, supervised by Prof. Angel Lozano. Before that, I was a postdoctoral scholar with Prof. Thomas Marzetta for two years in the NYU Wireless group at New York University, USA. I received my Ph.D. degree in Information Engineering at the University of Pisa, Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Luca Sanguinetti.

My research lies at the intersection of communications, signal processing and electromagnetism.
